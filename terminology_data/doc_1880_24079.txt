Curriculum vitae 
Sofia Trypanagnostopoulouhas studied Translation in the Department of Foreign Languages, 
Translation and Interpretation of Ionian University and Lexicography at the Department of English 
Language and Literature of University of Athens.  
She has worked in various translation and lexicographic projects.  She is currently a PhD student 
at the department of Language Sciences and Translation of PompeuFabra University of 
Barcelona.  
Her thesis subject deals with the representation of phraseology in English – Greek dictionaries.  
She works in the Human Resources Department of Doctors Without Borders – Spanish section
