Curriculum vitae 
Born in Athens, Greece. Studied Greek and French Language and Literature at the University of 
Athens. Post-graduate studies in France: Linguistics, Language Engineering and Technical 
Translation.  
Worked for the Greek Embassy in France, for the Laboratoire de Langues et Civilisations à 
Tradition Orale (CNRS) in Paris and for the Institute for Language and Speech Processing in 
Athens.  
Assοciate Professor of Linguistics at the University of Cyprus, Dept. of Byzantine and Modern 
Greek Studies, since 2002. Areas of interest: General Linguistics, Lexicography, LSP Translation 
& Terminology.
