Curriculum vitae 
Jan Roukens is a Dutch national living in Brussels, Belgium. He was born in 1937 in what is 
now Indonesia.  
He studied physics and was involved in health/medical informatics R&D before joining the 
European Commission (1981).  
For the EC he administered ITC R&D programmes and subsequently developed language-
programmes such as the multilingual information society programme (1996).   
He retired in 2002 and is board member of several language and terminology organisations 
since.  
He also has a particular interest for science communication and publishing.
