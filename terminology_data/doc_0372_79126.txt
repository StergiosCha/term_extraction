The Hellenic Society for Terminology (ELETO), the European Association for Terminology (EAFT), the Kapodistrian University of Athens and the University of Cyprus organized a conference on:
“National Languages and Terminology in Higher Education,
Science & Technology”
The conference was held on
7 November 2013
at University of Athens, Kostis Palamas Building, Athens, Greece.
The Symposium was supported by:
the Hellenic Society for Terminology (ELETO)
the European Association for Terminology (EAFT-AET)
