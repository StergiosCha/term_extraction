Curriculum vitae 
 
In September 2004 Benedetta Bertozzi graduated in Translation and Liaison Interpreting at S.S.L.M.I.T.- 
University of Bologna.  
In October 2004 she enrolled to the postgraduate course in Conference Interpreting at the above-mentioned 
university. Since 2004 Benedetta Bertozzi has been working as a free-lance interpreter and translator and in 2006 
she served her 3-month apprenticeship at the Italian-Hellenic Chamber of Commerce of Thessaloniki.
