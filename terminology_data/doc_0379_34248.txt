To become a member of ELETO please complete the following steps:
Read the
Statutes
Fill in the application form. The form is available in the following formats:
Application Form RTF
Application form PDF
Photocopy or scan the certificates to be submitted
Send the documents either by post to:
Hellenic Society for Terminology (ELETO), Syngrou Ave. 63, 11745 ATHENS, GREECE
or by e-mail to:
ELETO’s e-mail address
After your application is received, it will be examined by ELETO’s Administrative Board during their next meeting. You will be notified about the approval of your application both by e-mail and by post.
Finally,
after the notification above
, in order that your registration as a member of ELETO be effected, you should pay both the registration fee (5 €) and the current year annual contribution (30 € or 15 € for students).
