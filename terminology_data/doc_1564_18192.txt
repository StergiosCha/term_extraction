EAFT–ELETO Symposium 
“National Languages and Terminology  
in Higher Education, Science & Technology” 
Athens, Greece, 7 November 2013 
 
Kostas E. Valeontis 
 
Born in Pagondas, Samos, Greece (1944); degree in Physics from the University of Athens (1968); 
post-graduate Certificate of Studies from the Computer Applications Centre of the Hellenic Physical 
Society (1971-1972); post-graduate Certificate in Electronics and Radio Science from Athens 
University (1972). 
Research as well as teaching member of Physics Laboratory of the University of Athens (1969-1972). 
Member of research and development staff of the Hellenic Telecommunications Organization (OTE) 
for 30 years (1972-2001) – research and studies on matters such as telecom engineering, applied 
acoustics and standardization. Founder and leader of the "Acoustics Team" as well as the "Laser 
Communications Team” of OTE. Founder and chairman (since 1989) of the National Technical 
Committee "Telecommunications" (TE-T) and of the “Permanent Group on Telecommunications 
Terminology" (MOTO), being responsible for the national coordination of the telecommunications 
standardization as well as telecommunications terminology.  
Lecturer on «Applied Electromagnetics» at the post-graduate School of Electronics and Radio Science 
of Athens University for seven years (1977-1984). 
Author of papers, books, and terminology vocabularies as well as creator of terminology databases 
(such as Telecommunications term base TELETERM: http://www.moto-teleterm.gr/ and information 
technology term base INFORTERM: http://inforterm.cs.aueb.gr/), and lecturer in many seminars (on 
matters of acoustics, telecommunications, information technology, standardization, terminology etc.), 
also having translated several thousands of pages of technical documents (standards, technical 
regulations, techinical specifications etc.) on the above subjects. 
Founding member of the Hellenic Acoustical Society (EAKE) being its president for many years.  
Founding member of the Hellenic Society for Terminology, ELETO (www.eleto.gr), having been (1992-
2006) its Secretary General, (since 2006 till now) its President and (since 1995 till now) Chairman of 
its General Scientific Board.  
Lecturer on the «Principles of terminology and terminography» in the post-graduate course 
"Technoglossia" (http://www.ilsp.gr/texnoglossia/texnoglossia_eng.html) since 2002. 
Member of ELOT/TE21 technical committee «Principles of terminology», whose secretariat is kept by 
the Technical Chamber of Greece.
