The Hellenic Organization for Standardization NQIS/ELOT announces the submission for Public Enquiry of the draft Hellenic Standard:
EΛOT 561 – Ορολογική εργασία και επιστήμη της Ορολογίας – Λεξιλόγιο
The present draft Hellenic Standard ELOT 561:2024, has been prepared by the ELOT Technical Committee 21 (ELOT/TC21) “Terminology – Language Resources” and constitutes the Greek version of the current International Standard ISO 1087: 2019 “Terminology work and terminology science – Vocabulary”, modified to cover the Greek language, and includes, in Greek, terms and definitions of the basic concepts of terminology work as well as the equivalents of the terms in Greek, English and French.
