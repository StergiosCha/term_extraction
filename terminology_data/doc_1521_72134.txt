Curriculum vitae 
Konstantinos Skian is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at DaSciM, Ecole 
Polytechnique, Paris, where he also completed his Ph.D. thesis, supervised by 
Professors Michalis Vazirgiannis and Guillaume Wisniewski.  
His main interests focus on Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning and 
Data Science in general. More specifically he has worked in novel representations, 
regularization & distances for text classification.  
During his Ph.D. years, he has published and submitted high level papers in the most 
competitive conferences and worskhops in the areas of machine learning and natural 
language processing, like EMNLP, NAACL and NeurIPS.  
He has also participated in industrial projects with numerous companies and 
institutions, like AIRBUS, Paris School of Economics, ASIP Sante, INSERM and 
others.
