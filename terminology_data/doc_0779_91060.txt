Curriculum Vitae 
 
Natalya Voyevutko is Associate Professor at the Departments of ‘Greek Philology and 
Translation’ and ‘Pedagogy and Education’ of the Mariupol State University (Ukraine).  
She has a multifaceted authoring and research work with a sufficient number of publications 
in Ukrainian and international scholarly journals.  
She is the author of ten (10) books for students and pupils on comparative pedagogy, 
teaching Modern Greek as a foreign/second language, lexicography, and terminology.
