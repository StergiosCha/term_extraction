Announcement 
 
4th Conference “Hellenic Language and Terminology” 
 
The Hellenic Society for Terminology (ELETO) in cooperation with the University of Athens (UOA), the 
University of Thessaloniki (AUTH), the University of Patras, the Ionian University, the National Technical 
University of Athens (NTUA), the French Institute of Athens (IFA), the Hellenic Organization for 
Standardization (ELOT), the Institute for Language and Speech Processing (ILSP), the Organization for the 
Internationalization of the Hellenic language and the Panhellenic Association of Professional Translators 
organize the 4th Conference for the “Hellenic Language and Terminology”. 
  
The aim of the Conference is to present the current situation of the Hellenic Terminology in the multilingual 
environment of the European Union in relation with the requirements of the Information Society. 
 
The subjects of the Conference are the following: 
1. 
Historical aspects 
2. 
Linguistic-Ontological principles of Terminology focused on the Hellenic Terminology (principles and methods for 
designation, etc.) 
3. 
Terminological resources (special and multilingual dictionaries, term selections, special corpora).  
4. 
Standardization of Terminology (internationalization of concepts, and interlingual standardization and equivalence of 
terms). 
5. 
New technologies and Terminology. 
6. 
Terminology in Translation. 
7. 
Activities of organizations and bodies for Terminology. 
 
The Conference is open to everyone interested in the Hellenic language and terminology. The official 
languages of the Conference will be Greek, English and French. 
 
The Conference will be held in Athens on October 30, 31, and November 1, 2003. 
 
Participants wishing to present a paper at the Conference are invited to submit an abstract. The abstract 
should be written in one of the official languages of the Conference and should not exceed 150 words. 
The following items must appear on the page of the abstract: 
a) The title, place and time of the Conference: 4rd Conference “Hellenic Language and Terminology”, 
Athens, Greece, October 30, 31, and November 1, 2003 
b) The title of the paper 
c) The name, profession, full address, tel no/fax no and e-mail of the author(s). 
 
The abstract should be delivered up to March 15, 2003 either by e-mail (mail to: valeonti@otenet.gr) as an 
attached file in doc format (MS WORD 97 or WORD 2000), or by mail as a hard copy and a floppy disk, to 
the address:  
Hellenic Society for Terminology 
5, Socrati Tsakona str. 
GR-152 36  Penteli, GREECE. 
The authors will be informed about the acceptance of their proposed paper until March 15, 2003. Any 
additional information will be provided regarding the preparation of the final paper, which should be delivered 
in electronic form, before June 30, 2003.
