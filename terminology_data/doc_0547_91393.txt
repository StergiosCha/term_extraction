Curriculum vitae 
 
Irene Sotiropoulou is a heterodox economist with specialty in ecological, feminist, solidarity 
and non-capitalist economics. i.e. she researches and writes on economics which support 
social justice and ecological balance.  
She is currently working on several projects ranging from the economic analysis of folk culture 
(for which she has been awarded, with Dr Ferda Donmez-Atbasi from Ankara University, a 
Newton Mobility Grant by the British Academy) to heterodox monetary theories and medieval 
monetary history.  
She is a Fellow of the GEM-IWG/GEM-Europe and World Social Science Fellowship 
programs, and a Fellow of the Monetary Research Center (UNWE) at Sofia. Her writings are 
accessible at http://coventry.academia.edu/IreneSotiropoulou
