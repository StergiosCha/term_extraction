SNOMED Clinical Terms is a systematically organized computer 
processable collection of medical terms providing codes, terms, 
synonyms and definitions used in clinical documentation and 
reporting. SNOMED is the most comprehensive, multilingual 
clinical healthcare terminology in the world. 
SNOMED was started in 1965 as a Systematized Nomenclature of 
Pathology (SNOP) and was further developed into a logic-based 
health care terminology. It has been adapted and partially translated 
to 31 countries worldwide (not Greece, it seems).
