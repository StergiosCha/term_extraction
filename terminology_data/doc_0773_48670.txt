Curriculum vitae 
In September 1991 Mr. Franco Bertaccini became contract lecturer of the course “Literary Translation form 
French into Italian” at SSLiMIT of the University of Bologna.  
In 1993 thanks to the meeting with Guy Aston, Franco Bertaccini started to work in computational linguists, in 
particular in the quantitative analysis of texts and in the statistical analysis of technical texts, then he devoted to 
technical translation and to terminological and terminographic research.  
In 1996, together with Marcello Soffritti and Michèle Lorgnet, Franco Bertaccini launched the project “Linguaggi e 
attività produttive” and he is the coordinator of the University relations with business world.  
At present, at the S.S.L.M.I.T. in Forlì, in addition to the Terminological Project, he deals with terminology for 
integrated translation and he is the chief of the Terminology Research Laboratory website that today is an 
important online resource for translators, interpreters, terminologists and terminographers.
