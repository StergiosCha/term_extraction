Jacques Delors
EC president from 1985 - 1995
Perhaps it is time that the Europeans will 
identify those responsible for the 
fragmentation and the transformation of 
Europe into Eurabia. Maybe it's time, 
especially in an era of globalisation, to 
recognise the boundaries of an economic and 
materialistic view of politics. So that the 
precious values of humanity, which Europe 
even during the darkest periods of its history 
always wanted to maintain, might spread.
