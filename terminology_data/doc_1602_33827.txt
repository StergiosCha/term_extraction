Curriculum Vitae 
 
 
Ms Foteini Kalamida is currently a PhD student at the Department of Philology of the 
University of Patras. She holds a BA in English Language and Literature (specialization: 
Language and Linguistics) from the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens and an 
MA in Linguistics from the Department of Philology of the University of Patras.  
She has been working as a teacher of English as a foreign language at the private and the 
public sector for eleven years.  
Her research interests include lexicology, lexicography, terminology, translation and foreign 
language teaching and learning.
