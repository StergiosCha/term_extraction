Βιογραφικό Σημείωμα 
 
 
Η Ευαγγελία Ντζάνη, MD, PhD είναι Καθηγήτρια Επιδημιολογίας στο Εργαστήριο Υγιεινής και 
Επιδημιολογίας του Τμήματος Ιατρικής της Σχολής Επιστημών Υγείας του Πανεπιστημίου Ιωαννίνων. 
 
Dr. Evangelia Ntzani, MD, PhD is an Assistant Professor of Epidemiology in the Department of 
Hygiene and Epidemiology, University of Ioannina, School of Medicine and an Adjunct Assistant 
Professor of Health Services, Policy and Practice, in the Department of Health Services, Policy and 
Practice, at the School of Public Health, Brown University, USA. She received the University degree in 
Medicine from the University of Ioannina (Ioannina, Greece) in 2000, and specialized in Pediatrics 
(University of Ioannina) in 2009. She has got a PhD in Epidemiology from the University of Ioannina in 
2004. She is author in 46 peer-reviewed scientific papers in clinical and molecular epidemiology and 
evidence-based medicine (4705 citations, h-index 18), more than 50 abstracts presented at national 
and international meetings and more than 10 reviews and chapters published in books or non-peer 
reviewed journals. She is participating in the European and Developing Countries Clinical Trials 
Partnership; The European Network of Centres for Pharmacoepidemiology and Pharmacovigilance 
(ENCePP); and the Bias Methods Group, Cochrane Collaboration. Her research interests include 
clinical and molecular epidemiology, assessment of large-scale clinical and molecular information, 
evidence-based medicine, and research methodology and bias.
