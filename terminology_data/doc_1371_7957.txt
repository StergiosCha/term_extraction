Curriculum vitae 
Born 1946 in Waldkirchen/Germany. Former profession: typesetter (in Germany 
and the Netherlands, 1960–1971).  After evening classes "Abitur" in 1972. From 
1972 to 1978 German and Scandinavian Studies and Nordic Folklore at the 
University of Kiel (Germany) and the Åbo Akademi (Finland).  Since 1979 teaching 
Finnish-GermanTranslation and Interpreting at the University of Tampere; since 
1996 senior lecturer. December 2005: PhD, Doctoral thesis, published as »Gut 
zum Druck«? - Typographie und Layout im Übersetzungsprozess (Ready for print? 
- Typography and layout in translation process). Other research interests include 
the professional translation process and translator training. Numerous papers and 
articles in the fields of translation studies, especially the relationship of professional 
translation process and typography.
