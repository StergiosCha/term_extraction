ELETO – 9th Conference «Hellenic Language and Terminology» 
Athens, GREECE, 7-9 November 2013 
 
 
Khalid ANSAR 
Researcher at The Language Planning Center, IRCAM, Morocco 
IRCAM, Avenue Allal El Fassi, Madinat Al Irfane, Hay Ryad, B. P. 2055, Rabat, Maroc. 
E-mail: aanssar@ircam.ma  
 
Curriculum vitae 
‐ 
PhD in Amazigh phonology from the University of Mohammad V, Rabat, Morocco. 
Title of the thesis: Sibilants in Berber. 
‐ 
Teacher at the English department at the University of Ibn Tofail, Kénitra, Morocco 
(from 2000 till 2007). 
‐ 
Researcher at the Language Planning Centre in the Royal Institute of Amazigh 
Culture, Morocco, since 2007. 
‐ 
Involved in the national project of inserting Amazigh in the educational system, by 
providing training for teachers, inspectors, students and associations. 
‐ 
Involved in the project of Planning Amazigh in Morocco. 
‐ 
Contributed in the accomplishment of a number of terminological works in the 
domains of education, administration and the media. 
‐ 
Wrote a number of articles on Amazigh phonology, terminology and language 
planning. 
‐ 
Involved in the teaching of various Amazigh subjects at the university level in 
Morocco. 
‐ 
Participated in some conferences, as a representative of Morocco, to shed light on 
the Amazigh language and culture. 
‐ 
Domains of interest: phonology, terminology, lexicology, lexicography, language 
planning.
