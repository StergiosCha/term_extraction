“National Languages and Terminology

Athens, Greece, 7 November 2013 
 
 
Marita Kristiansen 
Marita.Kristiansen@nhh.no  
Marita Kristiansen is associate professor of English linguistics at the Norwegian School of 
Economics (NHH) in Bergen where she teaches business English and terminology & 
specialised communication. She also coordinates the Nordic Termdist network which offers 
an online master course in terminology.  
 
Her current research interests include domain dynamics, neology studies, term formation 
and the use of online corpora to detect new Norwegian terminology. In addition, she has in 
recent years worked on language planning and policy issues both as a former member of the 
Norwegian language council's terminology advisory board and locally at her home institution 
in establishing language policies for NHH. Presently she is a member of the Norwegian 
Association of Higher Education Institutions' term group and has recently also participated in 
a Nordic network which focuses on domain loss vs. the internationalisation of Nordic higher 
education institutions (http://nordiskparallelsprogsnet.blogs.ku.dk/).  
 
She has also for several years worked with colleagues at NHH on developing a national 
terminology portal as well as language technology resources for terminology, including a 
termbase for economic-administrative domains. She has published papers in international 
journals such as Terminology, Studies in Corpus Linguistics, Neologica and Terminology 
Science and Research: Journal of the International Institute of Terminology Research.
