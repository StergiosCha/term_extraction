Curriculum vitae 
Sigmund Kvam is professor of German linguistics and translatology at the Østfold 
University College in Halden in Norway. He studied German, English and History at 
the University of Trondheim (Norway) and the University of Freiburg (Germany) 
and received his Dr. phil. in Comparative Germanic Linguistics from the University 
of Freiburg in 1981. He has taught German grammar, translation and LSP theory  
at the Østfold University College in Halden in Norway and translation theory and 
LSP translation from English into Norwegian at the University of Oslo. His main 
research areas are contrastive grammar, LSP theory and translatology, first of all 
connected to translation theory and text linguistic approaches to translation. 
Together with colleagues from Greece, Austria, Denmark and Latvia he 
participated in developing the LSP translation teaching programme Wisstrans 
which took place in Corfu in 2006.
