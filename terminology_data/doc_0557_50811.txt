Curriculum Vitae 
 
Alejandro García-Aragón obtained a Bachelor’s Degree in Translation and Interpreting at the University of 
Granada, and specialized in Modern Greek studies and Lexicography during his Master’s Degree in 
‘Advanced Studies in Translation’ at the same university, where he is currently a PhD student and 
research fellow.  
Major Fields of Scientific Research: Lexicography, Terminology, Specialized Translation, Knowledge 
Representation, and Modern Greek Studies.
