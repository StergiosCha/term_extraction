Short history of the Termbase SENSOTERM 
 
The Termbase SENSOTERM is quadrilingual (Greek, English, French and German terms) and was created, 
in 2023, by Kostas Valeontis, president of the Hellenic Society for Terminology (ELETO) and chairman of 
ELETO’s Scientific Board (GESY) in order to import all the English, Greek, French and German equivalent 
terms of the Alphabetical English-Greek-French-German glossary of terms of Sensory Analysis (286 entries), 
which was prepared by Kostas Valeontis, George Tsiamas and Malamatenia Panagiotou, based on the 
sources: ISO 5492:2008 Sensory analysis – Vocabulary και English-Greek-Glossary of terms and concept 
definitions of Sensory Analysis. The latter has been prepared by GESY in cooperation with the Consumer 
and Sensory Lab of the University of the Aegean. 
The development, operation, maintenance and feeding of SENSOTERM is performed by its creator himself, 
who has also the responsibility for the content of ELETO’s website. Searching via the Termbank of the 
website has been ensured by Nikos Valeontis, the IT administrator of the website. Thus, free accessing and 
searching the Termbase SENSOTERM is available on the Internet, via ELETO’s website.
