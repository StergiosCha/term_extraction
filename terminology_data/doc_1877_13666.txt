ELETO – 6th Conference «Hellenic Language and Terminology» 
Athens, GREECE, 1-3 November 2007 
 
 
 
 
Marianne  Katsoyannou, PhD 
Department of Byzantine and Modern Greek Studies 
University of Cyprus, P.O.Box 20537, 1678 Nicosia 
 
Curriculum vitae 
Born in Athens, Greece in 1958. Studied Greek and French Language and Literature at the University 
of Athens.  
Post-graduate studies in France: Linguistics, Language Engineering and Technical Translation.  
Worked for the Greek Embassy in France, for the Laboratoire de Langues et Civilisations à Tradition 
Orale (CNRS) in Paris and for the Institute for Language and Speech Processing in Athens.  
Assistant Professor of Linguistics at the University of Cyprus, Dept. of Byzantine and Modern Greek 
Studies, since 2002.  
Areas of interest: General Linguistics, Lexicography, LSP Translation & Terminology.
