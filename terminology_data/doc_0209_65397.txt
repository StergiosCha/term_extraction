The site is under continual development and enrichment.
There is much content matter to be uploaded (informative documents, studies, events, papers, …). The visitor may contact us for comments and helpful proposals. We try to speed up the rate of growth of the site, hoping that this site:
serves terminology as a scientific discipline in its interlingual dimension and
promotes the Greek terminology, thereby serving the contemporary development of modern Greek language.
