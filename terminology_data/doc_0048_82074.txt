August 16, 2007
8th session of the General Assembly of Infoterm
: 16 August 2007 at Brigham Young University (BYU), Provo (Utah, USA).
The Infoterm General Assembly took place in connection with the annual meetings of ISO/TC 37 and its subcommittees, from 14 to 18 August 2007 as well as the Conference on “Pragmatic Applications of ISO/TC 37 Standards” on 13 August 2007.
