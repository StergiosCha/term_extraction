ELOT/TC80/WG1 «Terminology» 
 
ELOT/TC80/WG1 “Terminology” was the Working Group No 1 of the Standardization Technical Committee 
ELOT/TC80 “Electromagnetic Compatibility”, which had operated under the Hellenic Organization for 
Standardization (ELOT), its terms of reference being to process the terminology of the Hellenic Standards of 
Electromagnetic Compatibility prepared by ELOT/TC80, but also to study systematically the EMC 
terminology. 
Members of the Working Group: 
 
Kostas Valeontis, physicist, electronic-telecom engineer, Chairman 
 
Nissim Benmayor, Mechanical-Electrical Engineer (NTUA) 
 
Dimitris Nikolopoulos, Mechanical-Electrical Engineer (NTUA). 
During its operation, 1996–2003, in 300 sessions, the group 
– 
dealt with EMC terminology issues of TC80 and its WGs, as well as other TCs and WGs whose 
subject was Terminology or Standardization, 
– 
particularly, processed and harmonized the vocabularies of terminology of 54 Hellenic Standards 
prepared by the TE80/WGs, 
– 
had close cooperation with TE-T (Technical Committee “Telecommunications”) and with MOTO 
(Permanent Group of Telecommunication Terminology), as well as with the Hellenic Society for 
Terminology (ELETO), 
– 
attended the relults of the terminology work of IEC/TC77/WG1 Terminology and harmonized the 
Greek EMC terms with any changes applied to the International Standards concerning EMC 
terminology, 
– 
had 6 editions of the General and Special Terminology of Electromagnetic Compatibility printed and 
disseminated by its chairman. 
 
All terms produced by the Working Group have been imported into the termbase EMCTERM, by its 
chairman, who created the termbase for this purpose and who offered it to be uploaded on ELETO’s website 
for free public access.
