Curriculum vitae 
Thanos Mentzelopoulos was born in Aigio, where he still lives til today, in 1970. He is a teacher of 
the English language with a degree from the Department of English Language and Literature of the 
National and Capodistrian University of Athens and a Master’s degree from the Department of Foreign 
Languages, Translation and Interpreting of the Ionian University. Since June 2007 he is a doctorand at 
the afore-mentioned Department.  
Having worked from 1995 to 2002 as a substitute teacher of English in Elementary Education, in 2002 
he was appointed teacher of English in Elementary Education schools, while from 2003 to 2005 he 
was attached to the administrative services of the Ministry of Education, participating in the 
organisation team of several Conferences and seminars held in Achaia. He also participated as a 
speaker in Pedagogical Conferences and as a coordinator of EU educational programs such as 
“Socrates/Comenius” and other environmental programs of the Hellenic Ministry of Education. He is 
an associate (freelance translator) of the “Periskopio” Publishing House.
