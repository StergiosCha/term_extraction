ABSTRACT 
 
Conference Language Diversity and National Languages in Higher Education,  
Ljubljana 2009 
 
 
Summary of the report 
 
The report will focus on the Ljubljana Declaration, which was adopted on the international 
scientific conference entitled Language Diversity and National Languages in Higher 
Education, held on the 19th and 20th November 2009 in Ljubljana, Slovenia, on some 
problems of the use of Slovenian in higher education institutions in Slovenia and Slovenian 
terminology.
