Curriculum vitae 
Stamatis Outsios holds a BSc in Physics from the University of Crete, an MSc in 
Electronics and Telecommunications from the National and Kapodistrian University of 
Athens and an MSc in Electronic Automation from National and Kapodistrian 
University of Athens. Currently he is a PhD candidate in the area of “Machine 
learning - Data mining”, at Department of Informatics, Athens University of 
Economics and Business, supervised by Professor Michalis Vazirgiannis. His 
research interests focus on Machine Learning, Deep Learning for Natural Language 
Processing, Artificial Intelligence, Big Data Mining and Representation Learning. 
He is working in the technical and vocational education, as Electronic Engineer and 
is excited to participate and contribute to 4th Industrial Revolution that will 
fundamentally alter the way we live, work, and relate to one another. He thinks in the 
positive way and believes that technological revolution as a complement to the best 
parts of human nature - creativity, empathy, cooperation, thinking - can also lift 
humanity into a new collective and moral consciousness based on a shared sense of 
destiny.
