Curriculum Vitae 
 
Irene Sotiropoulou is a PhD candidate at the Department of Economics of the University of Crete since 
February 2009.  
She has graduated from the Law School of National & Capodistrian University of Athens (NCUA) (1996-
2000) and she holds a MSc degree in International & European Studies at the Department of Politics & 
Public Administration of the NCUA too (2000-2002) as well as a Postgraduate Diploma in Development 
Economics at the University of Manchester (2002-2003).  
Her PhD dissertation is titled "Exchange networks and parallel currencies: Theoretical approaches and 
the case of Greece" and consists of both theoretical study and field research. Related publications are 
available at http://economics.soc.uoc.gr/html/static_content/econ_phd/EN/Sotiropoulou/scv.html.
