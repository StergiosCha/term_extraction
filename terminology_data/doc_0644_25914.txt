Curriculum vitae 
Keisuke KASUYA is Professor of Sociolinguistics at the Graduate School of 
Language and Society in Hitotsubashi University (Tokyo).  
His research interests include the macro-sociolinguistics, the discourse analysis of 
linguistic representation, and the comparative study of linguistic ideologies. He co-
edited Language, State and Power (1997) and What is Linguistic Imperialism? 
(2000)(both in Japanese).  
Among his publications are “Discourses of Linguistic Dominance: A Historical 
Consideration of French Language Ideology” (International Review of Education,
