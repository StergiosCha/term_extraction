ELETO is governed by an
Administrative Board
consisting of seven full members and two substitute members, elected every three years. ELETO’s central scientific body is the
General Scientific Board
(
GESY
) consisting of both the members of the Administrative Board and the representatives of ELETO’s Collective Members, and it may be expanded by invited members. An
Auditing Committee
consists of three elected full or associate members.
