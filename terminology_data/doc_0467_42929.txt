Curriculum vitae 
 
Kimon Papadimitirou received his graduate Diploma in Rural and Surveying Engineering from the 
Department of Rural and Surveying Engineering, Polytechnic School at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki 
in 1995.  
He completed a postgraduate Specialization on the Analysis and Management of Geological Risks at the 
University of Geneva, Switzerland, in 2000 and his PhD in Rural and Surveying Engineering at the 
Department of Rural and Surveying Engineering, Polytechnic School, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki 
in 2004.  
He has participated as a GIS specialist and cartographer in several research programs and he has been 
teaching at graduate and postgraduate students of Aristotle University of Thessaloniki since 2005.  
Contact: paki@topo.auth.gr
