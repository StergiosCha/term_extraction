Athens, GREECE, 7-9 November 2013 
 
 
 
Dr. Konstantina Liontou 
Ηλ-ταχ.: konstantina.liontou@yahoo.com  
 
Curriculum vitae 
Konstantina Liontou graduated from the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens (U.O.A.), 
Faculty of English Studies in 2003.  
In 2006 she was awarded a Postgraduate Specialization Diploma (MA) in “Language 
Technologies” after the completion of the Interdisciplinary – Interuniversitary Postgraduate 
Course “Technoglossia” organised by the U.O.A. and the National Technical University of Athens 
(N.T.U.A.).  
In 2008 she was awarded a Degree in Conference Interpreting by the Ionian University, 
Department of Foreign Languages, Translation and Interpreting (D.F.L.T.I), Corfu, Greece.  
In 2012 she completed her doctoral studies at the Centre for Translation Studies, University of 
Vienna under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Franz Pöchhacker.  
She has worked as an English language teacher in Greece as well as a freelance conference 
interpreter for German, English and Italian.  
Since April 2010 she has been an accredited interpreter for German and English with the 
European Institutions. Her main research interests lie in strategies of simultaneous interpreting, 
and in particular anticipation.
