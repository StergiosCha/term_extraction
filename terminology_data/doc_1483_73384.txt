ELOT/TC2/WG5 «Sound insulation» 
 
ELOT/TC2/WG5 was the Working Group No 5 of the Standardization Technical Committee ELOT/TC2 
“Environment”, which operated under the Hellenic Organization for Standardization (ELOT), its terms of 
reference being the preparation of Hellenic Standards of Acoustics, as well as the transposition of 
International Standards (ISO/IEC) and European Standards (CEN/CENELEC) of Acoustics, but also the 
systematic study of the acoustical terminology. 
Members of the Working Group: 
 
Kostas Valeontis, physicist electronic-telecom engineer, Chairman 
 
George Poulakos, physicist 
 
Sotiris Tesseris, chemical engineer. 
ELOT/TC2/WG5, during its operation, 
1. prepared the texts of 26 Hellenic Standards of acoustics, corresponding to International Standards or 
European Standards of Acoustics, 
2. processed the terms of 6 Hellenic Standards of acoustical terminology, based on International Standards 
of ISO and IEC, as well as on British and American standards, 
3. had 11 editions of the General and Special Terminology of Acoustics printed and disseminated by its 
chairman. 
All terms produced by the Working Group have been imported into the termbase ACOUTERM, by its 
chairman, who created the termbase for this purpose.
