Curriculum vitae 
 
He was born in 1968. He graduated the Department of Physics, University of Thessaloniki (1990). He 
was awarded the M.Sc. in Information Processing, Computer Science Department, University of York 
(1992).  
Having done his military service he attended a full year course of the Department of Environmental 
Studies at the University of the Aegean (1994 – 1995).  
From 1995 till 2003 he did research which led to a Ph.D. in Environmental Policy and Management, of 
the Department of Environmental Studies at the University of the Aegean.  
During the academic year 2000 -2001 he attended a postgraduate course on “Pedagogical use of 
Computers and Web in Secondary education” at the Department of Pedagogic and Psychology, 
University of Athens. He was awarded the Cambridge Proficiency in 1991.  
Has participated in several conference publications and participated in various international and Greek 
conferences.  
He works as a Computer Science teacher from 1994 and his current position since 2005 is a 
headmaster of the Second Chance School of Mytilini.
