Terminological databases
ELETO’s Termbank consists of the following terminological databases:
TELETERM
(Telecommunications – Resulting from the work of MOTO)
INFORTERM
(Information Technology – Resulting from the work of the group ELOT/TC48/WG1)
TERMTERM
(Terminology Science) – Resulting from the work of the group ELOT/TC21)
ACOUTERM
(Acoustics – Resulting from the work of the group ELOT/TC2/WG5)
ELECTERM
(Electrotechnology – Resulting from the electrotechnical standardization)
LEXITERM
(Telecommunications – English-Greek-French database from the quadrilingual dictionary of telecommunications terminology by J. P. Rehahn-ELETO)
EMCTERM
(ElectroMagnetic Compatibility – Resulting from the work of the group ELOT/TC80/WG1)
SENSOTERM
(Sensory analysis – EN-GR-FR-GE termbase based on the work done at GESY)
Search restrictions
The following restrictions apply when searching:
When searching a
whole word
there is no restriction.
When searching a
term part
you should enter at least 3 characters.
When searching an
abbreviation part
you should enter at least 2 characters.
