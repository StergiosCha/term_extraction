Curriculum vitae 
 
Ferda Dönmez Atbaşı is Associate Professor of Economics at Ankara University, Department 
of Economics.  
She is a graduate of Middle East Technical University (B.S. AND M.S. in Economics and 
Science and Technology Policy Studies respectively) and University of Utah (PhD in 
Economics).  
Her areas of interest are political economy, feminist economics, welfare economics and 
development economics.  
She is the Head of the International Economics and Development Economics sub-department 
and she teaches international economics and development economics at undergraduate and 
graduate levels.  
She has been awarded a Newton Mobility Grant by the British Academy for the project 
“Researching and teaching grassroots economics: A pilot project” (along with Irene 
Sotiropoulou).  
She is a Fellow of the GEM-IWG/GEM-Europe & GEM-IWG/GEM-Turkiye.
