Curriculum vitae 
 
Nicolas Karanikolas received his graduate Diploma in Rural and Surveying Engineering from the Department of 
Rural and Surveying Engineering, Polytechnic School at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki in 1997.  
He completed a postgraduate Specialization on the Design and management of local and regional development at the 
Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, in 1999 and his PhD in Rural and Surveying Engineering at the Department of 
Rural and Surveying Engineering, Polytechnic School, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki in 2005.  
He has participated as a GIS specialist and cartographer in several research programs and he has been teaching at 
graduate students of Aristotle University of Thessaloniki and department of Geoinformatics and topography of 
Technical Institute of Serres since 2005.
