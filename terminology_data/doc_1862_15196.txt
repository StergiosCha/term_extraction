Curriculum Vitae 
 
 
Panagiotis G. Krimpas is Assoc. Prof. of “Terminology, Translation and Legal Texts” at the 
Department of Languages, Literature and Culture of Black Sea Countries (Faculty of Classics and 
Humanities) of the Democritus University of Thrace (Greece).  
He has also given lectures at various Universities in Greece, Estonia, Romania, Ukraine, and 
Turkey.  
He is an advisory member of the Panhellenic Association of Translators (PEM), a founding 
member of the Hellenic Association for Translation Studies (EME) and of the Hellenic Network for 
Terminology (EDO), and a BoD member of the Hellenic Society for Terminology (ELETO).  
He is experienced as a special adviser to political institutions, lawyer and legal translator.  
He is an alumnus of the Law School of the Democritus University of Thrace, Greece (BA, LLD) 
and of the Institute of Applied Linguistics of the ‘Pompeu Fabra’ University in Barcelona, Spain 
(MA).  
He is fluent in many European languages.
