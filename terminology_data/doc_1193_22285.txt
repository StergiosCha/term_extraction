Curriculum vitae 
He was born in 1960. He is a graduate of Physics (BSc) (Queen Mary College - University of London) and 
also he has obtained a master’s degree (MSc) in Applied Acoustics (Kings College - University of 
London).  
He is a teacher of Physics in secondary education for over a decade. From 1986 until 1993 he has been 
employed in the National Deaf Institution of Greece as audiophysicist.  
During the period 1993-1994 he has successfully attended courses of the Dep. of the Environment of the 
University of the Aegean.  
He has also attended seminars on adult education.
