EAFT–ELETO Symposium 
“National Languages and Terminology  
in Higher Education, Science & Technology” 
Athens, Greece, 7 November 2013 
 
Delyth Prys 
d.prys@bangor.ac.uk  
Delyth Prys is Chief Editor of the Centre for the Standardization of Welsh Terminology at 
Bangor University, Wales, since 1993 and Head of the Language Technologies Unit there 
since 2001. 
She is a member of the BSI (British Standards Institute) Terminology Committee and also of 
the ISO (International Standards Organisation) Technical Committee for Terminology and 
other Language Resources. She is convener of the Socioterminology Group of that 
Committee.  
Her current projects at the Language Technologies Unit include two for Y Coleg Cymraeg 
Cenedlaethol (the National Welsh-medium College), namely the Standardization of Welsh 
Terminology for Welsh-Medium Education at Universities in Wales, and the Digitization, E-
publishing and Electronic Corpus project, which publishes out of print scholarly books in e-
format while simultaneously using them to build up a major research corpus of academic 
Welsh.
