ELETO – 11th Conference «Hellenic Language and Terminology» 
Athens, GREECE, 9-11 November 2017 
 
 
 
Kostas Valeontis 
Electronic Physicist 
President of the Hellenic Society for Terminology (ELETO) 
E-mail: valeonti@otenet.gr  
 
Curriculum vitae 
Born in Pagondas, Samos, Greece (1944); degree in Physics from the University of Athens (1968); post-
graduate Certificate of Studies from the Computer Applications Centre of the Hellenic Physical Society 
(1971-1972); post-graduate Certificate in Electronics and Radio Science from Athens University (1972). 
Research as well as teaching member of Physics Laboratory of the University of Athens (1969-1972). 
Member of research and development staff of the Hellenic Telecommunications Organization (OTE) for 30 
years (1972-2001) – research and studies on matters such as telecom engineering, applied acoustics and 
standardization. Founder and leader of the "Acoustics Team" as well as the "Laser Communications 
Team” of OTE. Founder and chairman (since 1989) of the National Technical Committee 
"Telecommunications" (TE-T) and of the “Permanent Group on Telecommunications Terminology" 
(MOTO), being responsible for the national coordination of the telecommunications standardization as well 
as telecommunications terminology.  
Lecturer on «Applied Electromagnetics» at the post-graduate School of Electronics and Radio Science of 
Athens University for seven years (1977-1984). 
Author of papers, books, and terminology vocabularies as well as creator of terminology databases 
(threee of which are: Telecommunications term base TELETERM: http://www.moto-teleterm.gr/ and 
Information Technology term base INFORTERM: http://www.eleto.gr/inforterm/), and Terminology term 
base TERMTERM: http://www.eleto.gr/en/termbases.htm) and lecturer in many seminars (on matters of 
acoustics, telecommunications, information technology, standardization, terminology etc.)  
Founding member of the Hellenic Acoustical Society (EAKE) being its president for many years.  
Founding member of the Hellenic Society for Terminology ELETO (www.eleto.gr) being its Secretary 
General (1992 - 2006) and President (since 2006), as well as (since 1995) chairman of its General 
Scientific Board (GESY).
