Curriculum vitae 
Christos Karatsalos has made BSc studies in Mathematics in the National and 
Kapodistrian University of Athens and in Informatics in the Athens University of 
Economics and Business as well.  
He has also made MSc studies in Computational Science in the National and 
Kapodistrian University of Athens.  
Now he is working in the industry sector as a Data Scientist.  
His research interests include Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, 
Computational Biology and Computational Finance.
